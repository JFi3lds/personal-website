Activities & Hobbies

Outside of school, work, and the Guard, I try to stay active, enjoy time with friends, and get out of the house. Here are a few things I like to do when I’m not working on labs or projects.

Going Out & Movies

I enjoy going places and doing things like catching a movie or trying new spots around town. It’s a good way to relax, reset, and keep life from feeling too repetitive.

Walking My Dogs

Walking my dogs gets me outside and away from the screen. It’s simple, but it’s one of my favorite ways to clear my head and enjoy some fresh air.

Sports & Gaming

I like playing sports with friends and staying competitive, whether it’s in real life or on my PS5. In my downtime I enjoy playing games like NCAA 26, NBA2K26, and MADDEN 26.

Going on Trips

I enjoy going on trips and getting out of the usual routine. Even short trips help me reset and come back with more energy for everything I’m working on.
